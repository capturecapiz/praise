About the PRAISE Awards

The Program on Awards and Incentives for Service Excellence (PRAISE) is an initiative by the Civil Service Commission, adopted by the Provincial Government of Capiz. It aims to recognize and reward officials and employees for their exceptional performance, innovative ideas, and unwavering commitment to public service. This program fosters a culture of excellence, integrity, and accountability, inspiring every public servant to contribute to a more effective and responsive local government.
